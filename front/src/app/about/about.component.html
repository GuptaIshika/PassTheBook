PassTheBook   Story

The books or novels one purchases to read, are kept in a shelf after they have read. They just occupy space in our shelves. So, we thought we to keep them as show piece? Let's read it and circulate it on the internet for others to read it and have some fun.

We are here to help you guys to make circulating easy. Register on the website and publish your book by following simple steps: Take a snap of your book, post it here, give a discription about it along with your details and done. Now whoever wants to read it can directly contact you and pick it up from you.
